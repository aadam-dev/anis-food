# Content Strategy

## Menu Content Structure

### Menu Item Information
Each menu item should include:
- **Name**: Clear, descriptive name
- **Description**: 1-2 sentences describing the dish
- **Price**: In GHS (Ghana Cedis)
- **Category**: Rice, Noodles, Sandwiches, Sides, Drinks
- **Image**: High-quality, appetizing photo
- **Popular Badge**: For bestsellers
- **Availability**: Real-time availability status

### Menu Categories
1. **Rice Dishes**: Jollof, Fried Rice, Plain Rice with stews
2. **Noodles**: Indomie and other noodle dishes
3. **Sandwiches & Snacks**: Spring rolls, samosas, etc.
4. **Sides**: Individual rice portions, accompaniments
5. **Drinks**: Beverages (to be added)

## Social Media Content Sync

### Content Types
1. **Food Photos**: Daily specials, popular dishes
2. **Behind-the-Scenes**: Kitchen, preparation
3. **Customer Photos**: User-generated content
4. **Promotions**: Special offers, discounts
5. **Events**: Special occasions, celebrations

### Posting Schedule
- **Instagram**: 1-2 posts daily
- **Facebook**: 1 post daily
- **Stories**: Multiple updates throughout day

### Content Themes
- Monday: Motivation Monday (food inspiration)
- Tuesday: Tasty Tuesday (feature dish)
- Wednesday: Midweek Special
- Thursday: Throwback Thursday
- Friday: Weekend Vibes
- Saturday: Customer Spotlight
- Sunday: Sunday Special

## Image Requirements

### Food Photography
- **Resolution**: Minimum 1200x800px
- **Format**: JPEG or WebP
- **Aspect Ratio**: 3:2 or 16:9
- **Style**: Bright, appetizing, natural lighting
- **Background**: Clean, uncluttered

### Restaurant Photos
- **Interior**: Wide shots showing atmosphere
- **Exterior**: Building facade, signage
- **Staff**: Friendly, professional photos
- **Events**: Candid, authentic moments

### Logo & Branding
- **Logo**: Vector format (SVG) preferred
- **Colors**: Match brand guidelines
- **Usage**: Consistent across all platforms

## Copywriting Tone

### Brand Voice
- **Friendly**: Warm and welcoming
- **Authentic**: Genuine, not corporate
- **Passionate**: Love for food and community
- **Accessible**: Easy to understand, not pretentious

### Writing Style
- Use active voice
- Short, clear sentences
- Conversational tone
- Include local references when appropriate
- Emphasize value and quality

### Example Tones

**Homepage Hero:**
> "Experience the rich flavors of Ghana right here in Botwe, Accra. From our signature jollof rice to perfectly grilled chicken, every dish is prepared with love and fresh ingredients."

**Menu Description:**
> "Large portion of authentic jollof rice with perfectly grilled chicken"

**About Section:**
> "Anis Food and Drink was born from a simple passion: to bring authentic, delicious Ghanaian cuisine to our community at prices that everyone can afford."
